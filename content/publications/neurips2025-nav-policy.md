We present a reinforcement learning framework for optimal nap scheduling across multi-cushion couch environments. Our approach models cushion softness, sunlight angle, and belly rub probability as state variables. The resulting policy achieves 99.7% nap satisfaction rate, outperforming random nap placement by 40%.
