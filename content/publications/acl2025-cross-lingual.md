We introduce FetchNet, a lightweight neural network for predicting ball trajectories in real-time outdoor environments. By incorporating tail-wagging frequency as an attention signal, FetchNet achieves a 95% catch rate across varying wind conditions and throw angles, significantly outperforming classical paw-tracking baselines.
